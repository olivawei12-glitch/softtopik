What is TOPIK?

The Test of Proficiency in Korean (TOPIK) measures Korean language ability across six levels. It is divided into TOPIK I (Levels 1–2) and TOPIK II (Levels 3–6).

TOPIK I

Beginner level (1–2). Focus on vocabulary, basic grammar, and reading/listening skills.

TOPIK II

Intermediate & Advanced (3–6). Includes reading, listening, and writing sections.

Level Overview

Level 1–2: Basic daily communication
Level 3–4: Academic and workplace Korean
Level 5–6: Advanced fluency and formal writing
